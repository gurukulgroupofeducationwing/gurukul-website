Welcome to Gurukul Group of Education Wing

Gurukul Group of Education Wing is a premier CBSE-affiliated institution committed to delivering holistic education. We believe every student has unique strengths, and our role is to identify and nurture those strengths in a supportive environment.

Our experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and structured curriculum prepare students not just for examinations but for life. We combine academic rigor with co-curricular activities to develop well-rounded individuals.

  • CBSE Affiliated, Classes KG to XII
  • Experienced and dedicated teaching faculty
  • Modern labs, library, and sports facilities
  • Safe, inclusive campus environment

Our Facilities

🔬

Science Laboratory

Fully equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Students learn through hands-on experiments aligned with the CBSE curriculum.

💻

Computer Lab

Modern computer labs with high-speed internet. Students get practical exposure to programming, MS Office, and digital tools.

📚

Library

A well-stocked library with over 10,000 books, periodicals, journals, and digital resources for students and staff.

⚽

Sports Ground

Large grounds for cricket, football, volleyball, basketball, and athletics. We believe physical fitness is as important as academics.

🏠

Hostel

Separate boys and girls hostel with 24/7 supervision, nutritious meals, and a comfortable study environment.

🎨

Activity Rooms

Dedicated spaces for music, art, dance, and drama. Co-curricular activities are an integral part of our daily schedule.

Dear Parents,

It is with great pleasure that we welcome you and your family to our school, Gurukul Group of Educational Wing.
