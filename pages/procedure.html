Admission Process

Gurukul Group of Educational Wing welcomes students to an enriching and joyful learning experience. The admission procedure is completely transparent and has been designed to ensure a suitable fit between what parents and children expect from the school and what the school in turn offers them.

1

Obtain Prospectus & Application Form

The prospectus and application forms are available at the school office during office hours only.

2

Complete the Application Form

The Admission Application Form must be properly filled. Attach all necessary documents and affix the latest photograph of the student and parents. Please note that the institute reserves the right to refuse an application form if the candidate and parents do not affix a latest photo.

3

Admission Test

Students will be selected through an admission test only. Candidates who qualify the test will be called for counseling on a specific date.

4

Counseling

All original documents mentioned in the Admission Application Form must be produced during counseling.

5

Admission Confirmation

Students will be called for admission after successful counseling.
