Annual Week 2023

📅 1st – 8th December 2023  •  Gurukul Campus, Mangaldoi

The Annual Week at Gurukul Group of Educational Wing is a week-long celebration of student talent, hard work, and academic achievement. Events include inter-class competitions in drawing, essay writing, quiz, debate, sports, music, and drama.

All students from Classes LKG to XII are encouraged to participate. Prizes will be awarded at the Annual Day Function on 10th December 2023.

Parents are welcome to visit the campus during this week. Contact the school office for the day-wise schedule.

Annual Day Function 2023

📅 10th December 2023  •  Gurukul Campus, Mangaldoi

The Annual Day Function is the highlight of the school year. The programme includes prize distribution for academic and co-curricular achievers, cultural performances by students of all age groups, and special addresses by the Principal and Founder-Director.

All parents, guardians, and well-wishers of the school are cordially invited to attend.

Venue: Gurukul Group of Educational Wing, NA-Howly, Barathiabari, Mangaldoi, Assam — 784529.
