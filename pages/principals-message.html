Principal

Mrs. Sangeeta Adhikari Sarkar

B.Sc., B.Ed., M.Sc. (Mathematics)

Dear Parents,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you and your family to the Gurukul Group of Educational Wing.

At Gurukul, we believe that education is not just about academic excellence, but about nurturing values, character, and life skills. As Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam rightly said, “The purpose of education is to make good human beings with skill and expertise.” With this vision, we strive to create an environment where every child grows into a confident, responsible, and compassionate individual.

Each child is unique, and our dedicated teachers work with care and commitment to understand their individual strengths and needs. We aim to instill discipline, positive behaviour, and a lifelong love for learning in every student.

In today’s dynamic world, effective time management and strong collaboration between parents, teachers, and students are essential. Your continuous support plays a vital role in shaping your child’s future.

Let us work together to ensure the holistic development of our children. May this academic journey bring growth, happiness, good health, and wisdom to all.

Let us continue to learn, grow, and serve with sincerity and purpose.


With warm regards,

Mrs. Sangeeta Adhikari Sarkar

Principal, Gurukul Group of Educational Wing
