Mrs. Sangeeta Adhikari Sarkar

B.Sc. B.Ed., M.Sc. (Mathematics)

Dear Parents,

It is with great pleasure that we welcome you and your family to our school, Gurukul Group of Educational Wing.

As Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam said, "The purpose of Education is to make good human beings with skill and expertise... Enlightened human beings can be created by teachers." So, a teacher plays a key role in building skills, good qualities, behaviour, and healthy habits among children. Teachers need to deal with individual differences in order to make learning easier. Success depends on education of its people.

Effective time management is the need of the hour — for the student, the teacher, and the parent.

Let us all contribute to the all-round development of the child and be enriched with learning experiences. I hope the session brings each one of us joy, good luck, health, and wisdom. Let us serve others willingly, graciously, and selflessly.


With warm regards

Mrs. Sangeeta Adhikari Sarkar

Principal, Gurukul Group of Education Wing
