Welcome to Gurukul

Gurukul Group of Educational Wing is a co-educational school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi vide affiliation No. 230192. The school offers a choice between Residential and Day Scholastic Programmes.

Established to make children well-rounded and self-dependent individuals, the school has been constructed on a sprawling campus with splendid infrastructural facilities in a tranquil and pollution-free environment.

A comprehensive academic and co-curricular programme, experienced faculty members, personal attention and care, and proper infrastructure collectively ensure that the education a child receives at GGEW is truly complete. The school is a flagship project of Durlav Foundation, a non-profit organization vide Reg. No. DAR/247/K/46 of Govt. of Assam, with the goal of providing quality education.

Aims and Objectives

  • To provide Gurukulians a good atmosphere for learning through well-experienced and qualified teachers.
  • To cherish their dream for higher education.
  • Proper career counseling based on the candidate's basic concept to be Engineers (IITs, NITs), Doctors (NEET, AIIMS), IAS, IPS, IRS, IFS, Scientists, etc.
  • The teaching process is modified so that it will be easily accessible to Gurukulians.
  • To develop confidence, knowledge, self-efficiency, virtue, and discipline through experienced qualified teachers.
  • To nourish the Gurukulians into a particular shape and promote their knowledge to achieve their goal.
  • To provide education that transforms lives, improves society, and provides a foundation for a lifetime of learning.
  • To provide an enriched learning environment for every student.
