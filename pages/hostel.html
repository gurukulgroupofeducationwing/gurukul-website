Residential Programme

Gurukul Group of Educational Wing is a co-educational school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi vide affiliation No. 230192. The school offers a choice between Residential and Day Scholastic Programmes, established to make children well-rounded and self-dependent individuals.

The school has been constructed on a sprawling campus with splendid infrastructural facilities in a tranquil and pollution-free environment, making it an ideal setting for residential students.

Rules and Regulations for Hostellers

All residents are required to follow these rules. Violations may result in penalties, fines, or expulsion from the hostel.

  1. 1. Smoking, alcohol, and use of psychotropic drugs and substances will not be allowed in the school and hostel premises.
  2. 2. No parents or guardian of a student will be permitted to stay in the hostel.
  3. 3. Hostellers shall not remain absent from their hostels during 12:30 PM to 6:00 PM without prior permission of the warden / hostel superintendent.
  4. 4. Outsiders will not be permitted in the hostel premises.
  5. 5. Inmates shall not leave the hostel premises on holidays for excursions, picnics, or any other voyages without prior permission of the warden / hostel superintendent. The institution or hostel authorities bear no responsibility for any accident or fatality that may occur during such trips.
  6. 6. No resident is permitted to take any article or utensil outside the hostel / mess premises or into their room.
  7. 7. No association of students on the basis of religion, caste, or creed will be permitted.
  8. 8. No secret activities or meetings are permitted in the hostel.
  9. 9. Residents shall refrain from any act, covert or overt, that may destroy or disturb the peace and harmony among the residents and on the campus.
  10. 10. Keeping lethal weapons like sticks, rods, chains, knives, or any other prohibited materials in the hostel rooms will be strictly dealt with.
  11. 11. In case a student falls sick, they must immediately contact the warden / hostel superintendent. A student suffering from infectious or contagious diseases will not be permitted to stay in the hostel.
  12. 12. Mobile phones, laptops, or any other electronic gadgets will not be permitted inside the hostel. If found, a heavy penalty will be imposed by the authorities.
  13. 13. Fans and lights must be switched off when students leave their rooms. If fans or lights are found on in an unoccupied room, a heavy penalty will be imposed for wasting energy.
  14. 14. Every student residing in the hostel must dine in the hostel mess only. Individual or group cooking in the rooms is strictly forbidden and will result in expulsion or a fine.
  15. 15. Writing slogans, obscene text, or drawings on the hostel walls and rooms is prohibited. Heavy penalties will be imposed on students involved in such activities.
  16. 16. Consumption, storage, or supply of liquor or any intoxicant, or gambling, is strictly prohibited. Offenders will be dealt with severely, including possible prosecution.
  17. 17. If any violation of hostel rules is found, the parents and students concerned will be held responsible for the act and the authorities will have full permission to deal with the situation.
