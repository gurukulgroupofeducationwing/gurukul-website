Founder & Director

Dr. Durlav Sarkar

B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, Ph.D

It is a matter of immense happiness and pride on my part to write a few lines as the Head of this great institution, Gurukul Group of Educational Wing, at this stage of my life by the grace of Almighty who is the Creator of this vast universe.

"Education" — spreading of "Knowledge" which bears the meaning of "power" — has a great impact on the society in which we live. We know that "A pen is mightier than a sword." Education in the true sense of the term can be imparted only by an institute of great repute.

Gurukul Group of Educational Wing is such an institution where all the basic needs, particularly building character with attractive behaviour in regard to moral, intellectual and spiritual aspects, are being imparted with utmost care by highly qualified faculties in different branches of study.

To my estimation and long experience, I have come to know that the "Teaching profession" is the noblest and most respected profession in the world. Our faculty members, being sufficiently qualified in this field, are also rendering their level best to mould the character of students.

The authority has entrusted upon me the greatest responsibility for the upliftment of the academic performance of the students. Every one of us must think of the principle of 3H: (i) Head — we are to think what is to be done; (ii) Heart — we are to take the decision; and (iii) Hand — we are to do the things to materialise.

I do hope, with the active co-operation of teachers, guardians, and management and by the grace of Almighty, we shall be able to achieve our cherished goal which is a crying need of the hour. May God help us all.

The development of our country depends on the physical, mental, spiritual, moral, intellectual awareness and love for the motherland of our future generation. Our vision is clear, ambition is high, but our application is slow and steady. We hope to get success in due course of time.


With warm regards

Dr. Durlav Sarkar

Founder and Director, Gurukul Group of Education Wing
