Admission Process

⭳ Download Admission Form
1

Collect Form

Visit the school office or download the admission form from this website. Contact the school for the current session's admission dates.

2

Submit Documents

Submit the filled form along with required documents at the school office during working hours (9 AM to 3 PM).

3

Interaction

The student and parent will be called for a brief interaction with the Principal and class teacher.

4

Confirmation

Upon selection, pay the admission fee to confirm the seat. The school will issue an admission letter and ID card.

  • ✓ Passport Size Photographs (Parents)

  • ✓ EWS Certificate
  • ✓ Minority Certificate (if applicable)
  • ✓ Disability Certificate (if applicable)
